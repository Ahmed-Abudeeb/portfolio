## Overview

Senior design project addressing the environmental issue of plastic waste through an innovative gasification process to recycle plastic waste into valuable products.

## Approach

- Designed a gasification process to convert plastic waste into synthesis gas (syngas)
- Process converts syngas into green methanol and excess hydrogen
- Excess hydrogen utilized in fuel cells for renewable energy production

## Impact

This project demonstrated a circular economy approach to plastic waste management by converting a environmental liability into clean energy and valuable chemical products.
