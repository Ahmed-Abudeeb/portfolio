## Overview

Collaborated in a team project to apply artificial intelligence for kinetic modeling of a commercial naphtha hydrotreating plant.

## Approach

- Modeled hydrodesulfurization kinetics using real operational data from an industrial plant
- Preprocessed industrial data for AI applications
- Applied regression techniques to derive rate constants and catalyst activity functions

## Results

- Successfully demonstrated the application of AI methods to industrial kinetic modeling
- Derived meaningful kinetic parameters from operational data
